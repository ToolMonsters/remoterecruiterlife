Meet the Founder

Christine Spagnuolo isn't just a recruiter, she's a recruitment escape artist.
With 18 years in the talent game and an RPR certification, she cracked the
code to professional liberation in 2015.

While others climb corporate ladders, Christine relaxes in hammocks on
beaches. She's maintained six+ figures for 15 consecutive years while
systematically reducing her working hours – taking months off at a time – and
expanding her passport stamps. Working smarter not harder.

Her superpower? Matching talent so precisely that 85% of her clients hire her
first candidate. Companies like Ravl.io and Gowlings LLC don't call her for the
easy positions – they call when no one else can deliver.

From North America to Asia, she's turned "working remotely" from a secret
covert operation into an enviable lifestyle. Now she's showing others how
to pull off the same vanishing act from corporate America.
